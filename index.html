About

Empowering the future of interior design through cutting-edge 3D visualization and digital transformation.

The Platform for Next-Generation Design

Homestyler, a global platform jointly established by **Alibaba Group** and **Easyhome New Retail Group**, is dedicated to driving the digital evolution of the interior design and furnishing industries. We provide a comprehensive, efficient, cloud-based 3D design ecosystem for professionals worldwide.

  • **Advanced 3D Modeling Tools:** Offering high-fidelity visualization and rendering capabilities for complex projects.
  • **Industry-Leading Product Library:** Access to millions of real-world furniture and decor items for seamless integration into designs.
  • **Digital Transformation Solutions:** Providing specialized B2B services for furniture manufacturers and real estate developers.
